About Majlis Darbar Restaurant

Majlis Darbar ensures healthy environment. Make a short trip.

Centuries have passed but the authentic cooking style, memories are still with us. Step into the Majlis Darbar and travel back to the Arabian aroma and enrich yourselves in delicacies.

Our meal is carefully prepared by the talented & skilled chefs dates back to the Khaleeji tradition who bring each dish into perfection and a state of art with seasonal ingredients and wholesome spices.
